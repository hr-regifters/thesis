How it Works

Ripple connects all of your apps. You decide how they communicate.

Make Connections

Ripple is all about chain reactions. When you post a photo in instagram, we'll upload it to your dropbox. When you pin a message in Slack, we'll save it to your Evernote.

Build Concoctions

In Ripple, a relationship between two apps is called a concoction. A concoction follows basic cause and effect logic. Select a trigger app and event, then choose your action app and event. We'll take care of the rest!

Automate Your Services

Once you've made your concoction, we'll keep it listening in the background. Concoctions last as long as you want them to. When you know longer want to use a concoction, just click the disable toggle!

Easy Controls

With Ripple, your apps work for you, behind the scenes, even when you're not using them.

  • User Friendly

    It's easy to see what concoctions you have. It's just as easy to edit or delete them.

  • Write on the Fly

    Writing a concoction is easy, and you can do it on any device and any computer.

  • Discover and Share

    See what concoctions other users really enjoy using. If you've got one you use all the time, it's just as easy to share it with other users.

  • Connecting data

    Store all your fitbit and strava data in the same google spread sheet and consolidate your data.

  • Chain many actions

    If you post a photo to instagram, Ripple can save the photo to dropbox, send it in an email, and shoot off a text message.

  • User Friendly

    Connect with other users and keep your friends informed with automated text messaging and emails.
